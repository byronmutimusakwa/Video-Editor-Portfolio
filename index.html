About

I edit with DaVinci Resolve and Fusion, and I like spending real time with a project before I touch anything. Not just cutting to hit a deadline, but actually shaping the raw footage into something that has a pulse to it. I care about story structure: pacing that keeps people watching, cuts that mean something, motion and color that support the message instead of trying to show off.

Most of my work has been long form educational and entertainment content, mostly cybersecurity and hacking videos for YouTube. The real challenge there is keeping technical material genuinely watchable without losing the substance. That has taught me to think like a storyteller first and an editor second. How to build curiosity into a cold open, how to pace a fifteen minute tutorial so it never drags, and how to use Fusion to add just enough visual polish to make dense material feel cinematic instead of dry. I think about the audience from the idea stage too, not just at export. The title, the thumbnail, and the first five seconds decide whether any of the rest gets watched.

Skills: DaVinci Resolve, Fusion (motion graphics and compositing), color grading, story pacing and structure, sound design, retention editing for long form YouTube.
